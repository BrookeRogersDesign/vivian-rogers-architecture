About

Design as both conceptual and tactile

I'm Vivian Rogers, a designer and recent M.Arch graduate based in Los Angeles with experience spanning architecture, fabrication, and material research. My work lives somewhere between technical design and playful exploration — shaped by an interest in how people experience space, texture, and the environment around them.

With professional experience in architecture offices alongside hands-on work in wood, metal, ceramics, and digital fabrication labs, I approach design as both conceptual and tactile. I'm drawn to projects that balance personality with restraint and enjoy exploring ideas through making and storytelling across mediums.

Design Approach

How I Work

01

Research & Material Study

Every project starts with hands-on investigation — testing materials, studying site conditions, and understanding the systems already at work in a place.

02

Iterative Design

Through physical models, drawings, and digital tools, I develop proposals that are grounded in research and responsive to environmental context.

03

Synthesis & Storytelling

I bring projects together through clear visual narratives — connecting material logic, ecological thinking, and spatial design into a cohesive whole.
